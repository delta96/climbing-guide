The General

Grade: V6
Rating: 5/5
FA: Tim Rose

Information

Description

The General sit starts on a nice jug, making a shouldery move out right to a gaston,find your feet and go for a left side pull. Establish your feet once again and hit another right gaston. Get your feet up and shoot for the middle jugs. Once matched, get a high right heel hook and move left to a matched crimp. From there shoot on up to the top finding some hidden jugs.

Directions

Baron Harkonnen is located on the Jonah Boulder, on the backside of the boulder if looking up from the Squirrel Boulder. It's just left of The Belly, even sharing a couple holds.
