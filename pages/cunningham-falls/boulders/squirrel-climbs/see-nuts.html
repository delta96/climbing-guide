I Can See Your Nuts

Grade: V1+
Rating: 1/5
FA: Ryan Mallory

Information

Description

Stand start on a far right crimp and a far right sloppy hold. Get a high left foot and stand up. There are some hidden, savior crimps along the way to the top. Once you reach the lip, it is just a matter of pulling the mantle.

Directions

I Can See Your Nuts is located on the Squirrel Boulder, on the opposite side of all the main climbs. It's the first climb visible from the trail, if you hiked up from the parking lot.
