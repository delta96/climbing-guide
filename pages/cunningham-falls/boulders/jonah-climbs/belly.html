The Belly

Grade: V5
Rating: 4.5/5
FA: Unknown

Information

Description

The Belly sit starts with a left hand undercling and a right hand side pull towards the right face. Find a couple good foot chips and pull up right to a good jug. From there work your way up the arete and top out right.

Directions

The Belly is located on the Jonah Boulder, on the backside of the boulder if looking up from the Squirrel Boulder. It's just right of Baron Harkonnen, on the prominent arete.
