What's the Plural of Doofus

Grade: V3
Rating: 3/5
FA: Ian Hall

Information

Description

What's the Plural of Doofus sit starts on two credit card crimps with some tricky foot beta. Once established, pull hard to a right hand ledge, catch a left crimp, and throw up to the lip holding a possible swing. This is the perfect first V7 for the gym rats. Short, powerful, with some sick movements.

Directions

Baron Harkonnen is located on the Jonah Boulder, on the backside of the boulder if looking up from the Squirrel Boulder. It's just left of The Belly, even sharing a couple holds.
